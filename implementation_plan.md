# Mallouka Motors — Company Implementation Plan

> **Project:** Corporate Landing Page
>
> **Technology:** Next.js (LTS) + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
>
> **Purpose:** Build a modern, premium, responsive, SEO-optimized landing page representing Mallouka Motors while preserving the historical identity of Allo Casse Auto.
> **Languages:** English - French - Arabic (it should support those Three Main Languages correctly and the switching between those languages should be super smooth)
>

---

# 1. Project Overview

This project consists of designing and developing the official landing page for **Mallouka Motors**, a Tunisian automotive company specialized in imported used engines, gearboxes, and automotive spare parts.

The website should reflect professionalism, trust, experience, and modernity while maintaining the historical identity that thousands of customers still recognize as **Allo Casse Auto**.

The goal is **not** to create an e-commerce platform.

The objective is to create a premium company website that presents the business, showcases its services, builds customer trust, and encourages visitors to contact the company.

## Branding Strategy

The company has evolved through three different identities over more than twenty years.

Timeline:

• 2003 → Marsa Casse

↓

• 2012–2013 → Allo Casse Auto

↓

• 2020 → Present → Mallouka Motors

Although the official business name today is **Mallouka Motors**, thousands of existing customers still know and search for the company using the name **Allo Casse Auto**.

The company's physical storefront still displays the historical **Allo Casse Auto** branding, making it one of the most recognized names in the local automotive spare parts market.

For this reason:

- Mallouka Motors should be presented as the official brand.
- Allo Casse Auto should be presented as the heritage/public brand.
- Marsa Casse should only appear inside the company history.

Recommended hero wording:

> Mallouka Motors
>
> Known for years as Allo Casse Auto.
>
> Trusted Automotive Specialists Since 2003.

- Professional

- Excessive animations
- Bright flashy colors
